Design websites with AI.
Ship them with one command.

Stitch Forge turns Google Stitch into a full design-to-deploy pipeline. Generate screens, build design systems, and export to any framework — all from the terminal.

zsh — stitch-forge
$ forge design "Acme, SaaS, modern"
Generating brand architecture... done.
$ forge generate "Landing page"
Assembling components... 100% complete.
$ forge build --framework nextjs
