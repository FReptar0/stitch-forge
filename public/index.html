What Design Guard Does

smart_toy

Design Intelligence Agent

Automatically researches your business, analyzes competitors, and generates a comprehensive DESIGN.md blueprint before a single line of code is written.

Status: ACTIVE
auto_awesome

Screen Generation

Optimized prompting layer translates design intent into high-fidelity Stitch components automatically.

rule

Anti-Slop Validation

Proprietary heuristics engine catches common AI patterns and "slop" to ensure human-grade quality.

rocket_launch

Multi-Framework Build

Export your site to production-ready Next.js, Astro, or clean Static HTML/CSS in seconds.

Three Steps to Ship

From brand brief to deployment in record time.

1

dg discover

Agent analyzes your business goals and generates a DESIGN.md and THEME.json file defining your brand identity.

# Analyzing...
Output: brand_brief.md
2

dg generate

Claude orchestrates with Google Stitch to generate pixel-perfect screens based on your brand blueprint.

# Generating...
Stitching: index.html (98%)
3

dg build

One command to export optimized, accessible, and high-performance code ready for Vercel or Netlify.

# Building...
Success: site_bundle.zip
